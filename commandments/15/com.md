# 15: Fumes = Not good. Avoid them. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Printing PLA and PETG does not release fumes. ABS does.

Why: I think this one speaks for itself. If you are printing any styrene (ABS/ASA) or most engineering materials (PC/PA6/PA12), they can release toxic fumes. 

If you must print these, do so with an enclosed printer with good ventilation.
